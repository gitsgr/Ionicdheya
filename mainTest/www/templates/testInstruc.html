Intructions

  • This test builds the inventory of your likes and dislikes.
  • This will give you guidance regarding the vocation or subject that you like.
  • This test comprises of 72 statements, each with 2 options (L) Like and (D) Dislike.
  • You need to read every statement carefully and choose your answer option, if you like the statement choose (L) and if you dislike then choose (D).
  • There are no right or wrong answers. Every answer is correct for the individual.
  • Do not take too much time in answering these questions, give the 1st natural answer that comes to your mind.
  • Do not SKIP or OMIT any statement.
